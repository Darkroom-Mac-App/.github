## What is Darkroom?  

**Darkroom** is a full-featured photo editor designed to provide both professional and casual users with **fast and precise image adjustments**. It supports RAW, JPEG, and HEIC files, enabling users to enhance color, exposure, contrast, and more without leaving a streamlined interface.  

The app offers **non-destructive editing**, so you can experiment with creative changes without altering the original file. Darkroom also features **curves, selective color adjustments, batch processing, and custom filters**, giving photographers full control over the look and feel of their images.  

Users can **organize their photo library**, sort collections, and sync edits seamlessly with iCloud or other cloud services, ensuring consistent workflows across devices. The software also integrates with macOS system-wide gestures and shortcuts, providing a **fluid, intuitive editing experience**.  

Optimized for both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, Darkroom handles high-resolution images efficiently, making it perfect for editing large batches or working with professional DSLR photos.  

Whether enhancing a single shot, refining a portrait series, or preparing images for social media or print, **Darkroom** combines speed, flexibility, and power in one macOS-native editor.
